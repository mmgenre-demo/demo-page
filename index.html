MMGenre: Benchmarking Singing Voice Synthesis across Musical Genres

Overview

This demo page presents qualitative benchmark results of singing voice synthesis evaluated on MMGenre, a multi-genre SVS benchmark covering 10 musical genres. The audio samples are provided to support perceptual comparison across genres.

Benchmark Audio Samples

For each genre, we compare Suno ground-truth (GT) samples with samples generated by the SVS model (VISinger2). The synthesized samples exhibit relatively consistent singing style across different genres, with limited genre-specific variation.

This page is for anonymous review purposes only.
